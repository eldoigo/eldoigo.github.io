Our Story

A Hull Institution
for Over 25 Years

Dominic's has been serving the people of Anlaby Common and the wider Hull area since the late 1990s. What started as a passion for authentic Italian cooking has grown into one of the most trusted and well-loved takeaways in the region.

Every pizza base is hand-stretched from fresh dough. Every pasta dish is made to order. Our BBQ meats are marinated in-house and cooked to order — no shortcuts, ever.
